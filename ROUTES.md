## Alias routes supported

Several alias paths used inside the attached screens are also wired, including variations such as:

- `/faithhub/provider/donations-funds`
- `/faithhub/provider/replays-clips`
- `/faithhub/provider/reviews-moderation`
- `/faithhub/provider/charity-crowdfund`
- `/faithhub/provider/merchandise`
- `/faithhub/provider/new-merchandise`
- selected `new`, `review`, `rota`, and `publish` subpaths that should resolve back to the matching workspace screen
